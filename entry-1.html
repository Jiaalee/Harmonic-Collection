Steps of Fortune Reading in Tarot

Set the Mood: Create a peaceful atmosphere for your reading.
Shuffle the Deck: Mix the cards while focusing on your question.
Select a Spread: Choose a layout for the cards based on your question.
Draw the Cards: Pick the cards either randomly or as per the spread.
Interpret the Cards: Examine each card's symbolism and meaning in relation to your question.
Trust Your Intuition: Listen to your instincts and impressions.
Consider Card Combinations: See how cards interact with each other in the spread.
Tell the Story: Weave the card meanings into a coherent narrative.
Ask for Clarification: Draw extra cards if needed for clarity.
Reflect and Take Action: Use the insights to make informed decisions in your life.

Tarot cards, known for their ability to unravel the mysteries of the past, present, and future, are a captivating tool for divination.

While they don't predict one's fate, Tarot readings serve as a mirror to life's complexity, offering profound insights into the human experience. Through symbolism and intuition, Tarot readers decode energies surrounding an issue, revealing insights into possible outcomes and hidden influences.
