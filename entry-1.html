Tarot cards, known for their ability to unravel the mysteries of the past, present, and future, are a captivating tool for divination.

While they don't predict one's fate, Tarot readings serve as a mirror to life's complexity, offering profound insights into the human experience. Through symbolism and intuition, Tarot readers decode energies surrounding an issue, revealing insights into possible outcomes and hidden influences.

Steps of Fortune Reading in Tarot

  1. Set the Mood: Create a peaceful atmosphere for your reading.
  2. Shuffle the Deck: Mix the cards while focusing on your question.
  3. Select a Spread: Choose a layout for the cards based on your question.
  4. Draw the Cards: Pick the cards either randomly or as per the spread.
  5. Interpret the Cards: Examine each card's symbolism and meaning in relation to your question.
  6. Trust Your Intuition: Listen to your instincts and impressions.
  7. Consider Card Combinations: See how cards interact with each other in the spread.
  8. Tell the Story: Weave the card meanings into a coherent narrative.
  9. Ask for Clarification: Draw extra cards if needed for clarity.
  10. Reflect and Take Action: Use the insights to make informed decisions in your life.
